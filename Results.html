Our Research Findings

This section presents the results obtained from the air pollution analysis conducted using GIS and WebGIS technologies. The outputs include spatial distribution maps of NO₂, PM10, and PM2.5, land cover change detection results, and population exposure analysis. These visualizations provide insights into atmospheric pollution patterns and their potential relationship with land cover dynamics and population distribution across the study area.

Land Cover Change Detection

Land cover change detection analysis revealed significant transitions in land surface characteristics between 2021 and 2023. Notable changes included urban expansion, deforestation, and agricultural land conversion. These land cover dynamics were spatially correlated with observed air pollution patterns, suggesting potential interactions between land use changes and atmospheric pollutant distribution.
